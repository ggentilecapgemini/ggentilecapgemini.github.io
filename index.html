How to use this tool ?

We have developed a GenAI Agent Team to help you gather as much information as possible from Capgemini's internal resources to support your activities around GenAI topics.

This team agent is actually made up of 3 agents: a solution expert, a reference expert and a global knowledge expert. These 3 agents discuss together to answer your request as precisely as possible, challenging each other's approach to create a cognitive reasoning. (You got it, this is not ChatGPT).

Please note: this team is here to help you find solutions, references or GenAI knowledge among the data available in the GenAI for Digital Engineering Sharepoint, and propose you concrete answers.

Example of a relevant question: I would like to built a commercial proposal for a client in the Automotive industry to support their production of Printed Circuit Board thanks to GenAI solutions. What could support this offer?
